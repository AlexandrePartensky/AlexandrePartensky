Hi, I'm Alexandre.

I recently graduated from the Master of Financial Engineering at UC Berkeley and from the Grande École d’Ingénieur program at ENSAE Paris. My interests lie in quantitative research and trading, particularly in statistical modeling, machine learning, and market microstructure.

Below are some of the projects I worked on during my studies related to quantitative trading and quantitative research.

- **Intraday-Volatility-Prediction-Midcap-stock** – Forecasting 30-minute ahead volatility in U.S. mid-cap equities using limit-order book features and lagged realized variance. Models include HAR, Random Forest, and stacked approaches.  
- **Stable-VAE** – Implementation of a stabilized variational autoencoder for dynamic factor models to capture nonlinear risk factors in cross-sectional equity returns.  
- **Trading_Simulation_RIT** – Development of trading strategies and backtesting tools in a simulated market environment.  
- **QRT-Data-Challenge** – Implementation of statistical arbitrage and forecasting models in a competitive research setting.  
- **MCMC-ExchangeAlgorithm** – Implementation of Metropolis–Hastings and exchange algorithms for parameter inference in log-linear models with intractable partition functions.
